3 days ago • 🎭 CREATIVE SUCCESS
Our school production of "Romeo and Juliet" was a HUGE success! 🎭✨ But more importantly, we used it as a platform to discuss consent, healthy relationships, and the importance of communication!
Instead of just performing the tragedy as written, we added a modern epilogue where Juliet and Romeo learn to talk through their problems instead of making impulsive decisions! The audience LOVED IT!
Art should reflect our values and teach important lessons. Why perform outdated relationship dynamics when we can show young people what healthy love actually looks like?
Special thanks to my co-director Bebe and our amazing cast! Even Cartman was surprisingly good as the Nurse (though he kept trying to sell snacks during intermission). Mr. Mackey said it was "a very positive message, m'kay!" and Principal Victoria is already asking about our next production! #TheaterWithPurpose #HealthyRelationships #StudentTheater #CreativeActivism #ProudOfMyTeam #ArtForChange

5 days ago • 🏆 VICTORY!
WE WON! The school board voted 6-1 to implement gender-neutral bathroom policies! 🏳️‍⚧️✊ This campaign started 3 months ago when I noticed some students were uncomfortable using the existing facilities. EVERYONE deserves to feel safe and included at school!
What started as a simple suggestion became a full research project, presentation to the board, community meetings, and partnership with parent advocates. THIS is how change happens - one step at a time!
Huge thanks to: • Kyle for helping with research and statistics • My dad (PC Principal) for guidance on policy language • Strong Woman (my mom) for teaching me about intersectional advocacy • All the students who shared their stories with me
When we fight for justice, we don't just win policies - we win dignity, safety, and belonging for our community. This is what leadership looks like!
Next campaign: Comprehensive anti-bullying policy with real accountability measures. The work never stops! 💪 #TransRightsAreHumanRights #VictoryForAll #StudentAdvocacy #InclusiveSchools #ChangeHappens #NextFight
