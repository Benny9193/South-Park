Tweek Bros. Coffee — Coffee So Strong, You’ll See Through Time

WELCOME!
Oh God—hi! You’re here! That’s great, that’s… that’s fine. Totally fine. We’re fine. We’re a family‑owned coffeehouse right here in beautiful South Park, Colorado, and we’ve been brewing our signature dark roast since before Harbucks tried to roll in and ruin everything. (They didn’t. We’re still here. We’re fine.)

Our beans are roasted just right—by which we mean “until they scream”—and brewed into a cup so bold it’ll make your eyelids vibrate. People say it’s “too strong” or “tastes like motor oil” or “made me hear colors,” but that’s just because they’re not ready for the truth. The secret’s in the beans. And maybe something else. Don’t ask.

Our Story

It all started when Richard and Mrs. Tweek decided South Park needed a coffee shop that wasn’t run by soulless corporate drones. We fought the big guys, we won (sort of), and we’ve been jittering along ever since. Our son, Tweek, helps out behind the counter—he’s the one shaking uncontrollably while making your latte. That’s normal.

Menu Highlights

  • Triple Jitter Latte — Three shots of espresso and a prayer.
  • Harbucks Slayer Dark Roast — Bitter enough to make you question your life choices.
  • Tweek’s Tremor Shot — Espresso so concentrated it might be illegal in some states.

Why Choose Us?

Because we’re real. We’re local. And we’re not afraid to admit our coffee might be… a little intense. Plus, we’ve got free Wi‑Fi, a bulletin board full of conspiracy theories, and the occasional totally innocent Yaoi art of Tweek and Craig (don’t ask, don’t tell).
