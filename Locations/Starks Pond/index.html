Stark's Pond

A quiet, reflective pond on the outskirts of town.

Quick Facts

Location
Outskirts of South Park
Activities
Ice Skating, Fishing, Contemplation

About

Stark's Pond is a serene and often-frozen body of water. It's a popular spot for ice skating in the winter and fishing in the summer. It's also a place where characters often go to reflect on their lives.

Notable Events

The pond has been the site of several key moments in South Park history, including the first appearance of Mr. Hankey, the Christmas Poo.
